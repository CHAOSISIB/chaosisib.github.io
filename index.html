About CHAOS

CHAOS 2026 is ISI Bangalore' s annual techno-cultural and educational fest : a convergence of technology, arts, competitions and learning.

CHAOS 2026 brings together students across disciplines for workshops, talks, technical challenges and cultural showcases.

Expect rigorous quizzes, strategic game-play, research talks, hands-on workshops and memorable stage performances across March 6 - 8, 2026.

A platform for students to showcase their talents, collaborate with peers, and forge meaningful connections.
