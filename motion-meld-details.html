Motion Meld

Dance Competition

Overview

Movement as art. Solo, group, or freestyle: dance becomes a language of pure expression on the ultimate stage. Motion Meld is where dancers showcase their creativity, technique, and passion in an electrifying competition!

Rules & Guidelines

Event Rules

  • Rule 1 - Each group can have a maximum of 6 members.
  • Rule 2 - The music should be submitted in .mp3 or .m4a format at least 3 hours prior to the event.
  • Rule 3 - The performance time is limited to 5-7 minutes for solo, and 8-10 minutes for group. Penalties will be applied for overruns.
  • Rule 4 - No profanity or inappropriate content is allowed.
  • Rule 5 - The participants must be present at least 30 minutes before their scheduled performance time.
  • Rule 6 - The decision of the judges is final and binding.
